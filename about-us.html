Why choose us?

We are a team of Agriculture professionals, expertise with various disciplines like Crop Production, Plant protection, Agri-Horti Specialist and creative minds working on a common vision. We believe a work culture which is diverse and open. Nutrigreens began as experiment on our farm. Realizing we could provide intense flavors, vibrantly colored, and nutrient dense microgreens to our local community we were captivated. Our team continuously in touch with new innovations in microgreens from sowing to harvest of the produce. Using urban farming techniques, growing in a protected environment with organically sourced seeds and materials; we provide luscious microgreens year round.



Microgreens can be grown from various seeds. Their taste can vary greatly depending on the variety. Microgreens are rich in nutrients. They often contain larger amounts of vitamins, minerals and antioxidants than their more mature counterparts. Microgreens deliver a concentrated dose of nutrients and beneficial plant compounds. As a result, they may reduce the risk of certain diseases.
